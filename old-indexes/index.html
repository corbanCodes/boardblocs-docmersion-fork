Reimagining how institutions present and manage information.

Upload your documents—policies, handbooks, compliance reports—and organize them by collection or topic.

2. Brand & Control Access

Add your logo, colors, and supplemental content. Set public or private access for each document or collection.

Users search with natural language and get instant, consistent answers—on any device, always accessible.
