How boards use BoardBlocs

From meeting prep to public transparency, here's how BoardBlocs fits into your workflow.

1

Before meetings: Prepare and distribute

Upload agenda items, attach supporting documents, and share everything with board members. They can review materials on any device and add comments or questions ahead of time.

2

During meetings: Stay organized

Follow the agenda in real time, access linked documents instantly, and capture notes or action items as you go. No more shuffling through papers or searching for files.

3

After meetings: Publish and archive

Post approved minutes to your public site with one click. All meeting materials are automatically archived and searchable for future reference.

4

Between meetings: Keep momentum

Use discussions to continue conversations, share updates via board news, and keep committees on track with their own dedicated workspaces.

Questions we often hear

How long does it take to get started?

Most organizations are up and running within a few days. We help with initial setup, data migration, and can provide training sessions for your team if needed.

Can we migrate our existing documents?

Yes. We can help you bring over existing meeting materials, policies, and archives so your historical records are available from day one.

Is our information secure?

BoardBlocs uses enterprise-grade security with role-based access controls. Board members only see what they're supposed to see, and sensitive materials stay protected.

What about board members who aren't tech-savvy?

BoardBlocs is designed to be intuitive. If someone can use email, they can use BoardBlocs. We also offer a mobile app for easy access on any device.
