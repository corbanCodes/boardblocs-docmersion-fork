THE PROBLEM

Why PDFs Fail Your Community

Traditional PDFs are accessibility nightmares. They fail screen readers, break on mobile, and expose you to ADA lawsuits.

Screen Reader Failures

Most PDFs lack proper tagging, making them completely inaccessible to visually impaired users who rely on screen readers.

Mobile Nightmares

PDFs don't reflow on mobile devices. Users pinch, zoom, and scroll endlessly just to read a single paragraph.

Lawsuit Liability

ADA lawsuits against public entities are skyrocketing. Inaccessible documents are low-hanging fruit for plaintiffs.
