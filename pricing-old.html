Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about DocMersion.

What is DocMersion and what problem does it solve?

DocMersion transforms static PDFs into interactive web pages, making documents like policies, handbooks, and compliance materials easily accessible and searchable. It solves the problem of communities struggling to find crucial information buried in unsearchable PDF stacks, which often leads to frustration and complaints.

How does DocMersion improve the user experience for our community members?

DocMersion features an optimized search experience and offers the latest translation options, ensuring inclusivity. Community members can ask questions and get instant answers, and even share individual URLs to specific policies—making information consumption more efficient and user-friendly.

What makes DocMersion unique compared to other solutions like BoardDocs?

Unlike competitors that often link out to separate, disjointed sites for policies, DocMersion integrates documents directly onto your website as native text. This seamless experience avoids confusion for parents, enhances security, and allows for immediate translation using SchoolBlocks' language tool, eliminating the need to create separate documents for different languages. Beyond policies, DocMersion is versatile enough to handle a wide range of text-heavy content.

Is DocMersion compliant with accessibility standards and does it offer translation features?

Yes, DocMersion is fully WCAG 2.2 compliant, ensuring accessibility for all users. DocMersion also includes the latest translation options for seamless multilingual support. With documents living as native text on your website, SchoolBlocks' language tool can translate content instantly—addressing a common client concern about translation.

Beyond policies, what other types of documents can DocMersion manage?

DocMersion is highly versatile and excels with any text-heavy content that has groupings. While perfect for policies, handbooks, and compliance documents, it's also ideal for curriculums and other extensive text-based materials—providing an interactive, engaging experience for navigating large amounts of content.
