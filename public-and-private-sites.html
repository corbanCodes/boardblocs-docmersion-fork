Policy Governance Model

The school board approves policies that provide general principles for guiding the management of the school district. The superintendent and administration develop regulations that support these policies.

Speaking with One Voice

After a vote is taken, all members speak with a single voice, regardless of individual views. This unified position gives clear direction to management and staff who implement decisions.

What, Not How

Policy governance boards define what they are providing, to whom, why, and at what cost—but not how. That's up to the superintendent and administration.

Community Input

The board interprets community needs and receives feedback on all sides of issues. By considering all public viewpoints, they fulfill their purpose as a united governing body.
