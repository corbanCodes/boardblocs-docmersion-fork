DOCUMENTATION CHALLENGES

Why Record Keeping Fails

Even organized boards struggle with documentation. These problems create compliance risks and waste valuable time.

"PDFs are inaccessible"

Screen readers can't read them. Mobile users can't zoom properly. You're failing ADA/WCAG 2.2 compliance without knowing it.

"We can't find anything"

Minutes from last year? Buried in email. The policy we approved in March? Nobody knows where it is. Search is impossible.

"Version control is chaos"

Which agenda is final? Someone edited the wrong version. Attachments are out of date. Nobody trusts the documents anymore.

"Minutes take forever"

Someone takes notes by hand. Transcription takes days. By the time minutes are distributed, details are forgotten.

"Public can't access records"

Citizens request meeting minutes. You scramble to find them. Open records compliance becomes a nightmare.

"Documents don't work on mobile"

Board members review packets on phones. PDFs are unreadable. They show up to meetings unprepared.
