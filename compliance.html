What is Digital Compliance?

Digital compliance means ensuring your website and digital documents meet legal and ethical standards for accessibility, privacy, and security. For public-facing organizations, this is not just best practice‚Äîit's the law.

Key Standards We Meet

WCAG 2.2: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines ensure content is usable by people with disabilities.

ADA: The Americans with Disabilities Act mandates digital accessibility for equal opportunity.

CIPA: Children's Internet Protection Act requires safe and responsible online environments in schools and libraries.
