WHY IT MATTERS

Your Board Needs Its Own Voice

Board-level communications are different from daily operations. Give your governance team a dedicated space to share decisions, updates, and announcements with stakeholders.

Transparency

Demonstrate openness to the public. Show what decisions are being made and why—reducing confusion and building trust.

Communication

Many boards don't have a communications team. A simple news feed keeps stakeholders informed without extra overhead.

Public Trust

Publishing news articles improves professional perception, stakeholder trust, and demonstrates active governance.

Separation

Keep board communications separate from daily operations. Your governance team deserves its own communications home.

CONTENT TYPES

Board-Level News, Not Daily Updates

This is NOT where daily operational news goes. Board News & Updates is for governance-focused, high-level communications.

Announcements

Official board announcements and public statements

Policy Updates

New policies, revisions, and governance changes

Meeting Highlights

Recaps of key decisions from board meetings

Funding Updates

Budget approvals, grants, and financial news

Legal & Compliance

Court rulings, regulatory changes, compliance updates

Leadership Updates

Superintendent updates, board member news

FEATURES

Everything You Need to Communicate

Simple, powerful tools to publish and manage board communications without needing a dedicated PR team.

News Article List

Recent articles with titles, timestamps, and categories. Scrollable, searchable, and easy to browse.

Rich Article Editor

Format text, add links and images, save drafts, and publish when ready. No coding required.

Categories & Tags

Organize news by type: Legal, Finance, Community, Policy, etc. Makes finding content easy.

Subscribe to Updates

Stakeholders can subscribe to receive notifications when new articles are published.

Permission Controls

Public can read, admins can publish. Optional committee-specific news categories.
