OUR STORY

From school websites to full board portals

BoardBlocs comes out of years spent building public-facing sites for schools and local governments. We saw the same pattern over and over again: agendas, minutes, and policies were scattered across PDFs, email, and half-finished portals.

We built BoardBlocs so boards could have one place that feels like a natural extension of their main website—but with the tools and privacy a real board portal needs.

Who we build for

School boards, public agencies, and nonprofits who answer to a community and need to show their work.

What we provide

A public board website, a private workspace for the board, and an accessible document center powered by DocMersion.

How we think about simplicity

If your board members need training to use it, we missed the mark. BoardBlocs is built to feel obvious on day one.

What "board portal" means to us

A board portal isnt just another login. For us it means your public and private board worlds are coordinated instead of competing systems.

Public-facing board site for meetings, agendas, minutes, live streams, and policies, all matching your primary brand.

Private workspace where drafts, committee work, and discussions happen away from email clutter.

DocMersion document center that turns key board documents into ADA- and mobile-friendly web content.
