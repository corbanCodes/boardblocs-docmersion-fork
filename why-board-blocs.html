WHY BOARD PORTAL SOFTWARE MATTERS

One place for your board website, documents, and discussions

BoardBlocs is modern board portal software that gives you a public-facing board site, a private workspace for your board, and accessible documents powered by DocMersion—all in one platform.

Board website + portal

A branded board site at board.yourdomain.com plus a secure portal for agendas, minutes, and internal work.

Accessible by default

DocMersion transforms policies and board packets into ADA-friendly, searchable web pages instead of static PDFs.

Built for real conversations

Avatar-based discussions, sentiment tracking, and notifications make it easy to see how your board really feels.

Public transparency and private collaboration

BoardBlocs keeps what should be public, public—and what should be private, private. Your community sees a clean, accessible board site. Your board sees a focused workspace.

Public board site with meetings, agendas, minutes, streams, and policies that match your main website's look and feel.

Private portal for early drafts, committee work, and internal conversations.
